Anti-Bullying Awareness

Don't Exclude Others

Excluding someone from a group or activity can hurt their feelings and lead to loneliness. Always be inclusive and kind.

Don't Engage in Cyberbullying

Sending hurtful messages or sharing unkind content online is harmful. Use social media responsibly and think before you post.

Say No to Physical Bullying

Physical bullying can cause serious harm and fear. Always resolve conflicts peacefully and seek help if needed.
