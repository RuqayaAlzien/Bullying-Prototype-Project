Anti-Bullying Initiative

Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive environment where everyone is valued and respected. We aim to combat bullying through education and support.

What is Bullying?

Bullying involves repeated aggressive behavior that creates an imbalance of power. It can be physical, verbal, social, or cyberbullying. Our goal is to provide resources to prevent and address bullying effectively.

Get Involved

As a student, teacher, parent, or community member, you can help by reporting bullying, raising awareness, and offering support. Together, we can make a difference.
