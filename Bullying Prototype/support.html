Tips to Handle Bullying:

  • Don't respond to bullies; ignore them and walk away if possible.
  • Talk to someone you trust about the bullying; don't keep it to yourself.
  • Keep records of any bullying incidents, including dates, times, and details.
  • Seek support from friends, family, or professionals when needed.
  • Stand up for others who are being bullied if it's safe to do so.

Resources for Help:

  • National Bullying Helpline
  • StopBullying.gov
  • Local Counseling Services
